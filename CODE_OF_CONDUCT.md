# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Be respectful, constructive, and considerate. Welcome people with different backgrounds and levels
of experience. Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people. Harassment, discrimination, threats,
doxxing, and deliberately disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces and to public interactions where someone represents
the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through the repository's
[security advisory form](https://github.com/YRashid/actual-schema-gradle-plugin/security/advisories/new).
Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept confidential as far as practical. Maintainers may edit or
remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
